Greek salad

Vegetarian recipe!

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Feta cheese
  • Kalamata olives
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Seasonings you enjoy

Directions

Let's split this into a few steps:

Prepping

Chop all vegetables.

Assembling

In a large bowl, combine all chopped vegetables. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and your favorite seasonings. Toss to combine.

Time to Eat!

Enjoy by itself or as a side dish!

Caesar salad

Contains fish - non-vegetarian!

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan
  • Croutons
  • Anchovies
  • Mayo
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Directions

Let's split this into a few steps:

Prepping

Chop romaine lettuce.

For the sauce, mix the mayo with chopped anchovies and grated Parmesan.

Assembling

In a large bowl, combine lettuce and sauce. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and black pepper. Add Croutons.

Time to Eat!

Enjoy by itself or as a side dish!
